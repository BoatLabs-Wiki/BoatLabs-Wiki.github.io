Concrete IO

  • Claim the most ground before time runs out! Drive out of your coloured zone to lay a powder trail, then loop back to your own colour to fill in everything you circled.
  • Your trail is your weak spot. While you're out laying powder, if anyone touches your trail you're eliminated — so close your loop and get back onto safe ground fast.
  • Cross an enemy's trail to wipe them out before they make it home. Anyone caught inside a zone at the moment you capture it is eliminated too.
  • Don't run into your OWN trail — a tiny backtrack onto your last few cells is fine, but crossing an older part crashes you. Stay off the arena walls.
  • Biggest territory when the clock hits zero wins, and survivors always beat anyone who got eliminated. Keep claiming!

Guess The Track

  • Each of the four coloured walls shows a different track — full image, no slow reveal.
  • Name any of them: /guess <track> (alias /g). Tab-complete the name so spelling never matters.
  • Keep going to claim every wall. Faster = more points (5→1 as the round ages), +1 for claiming a wall first.
  • From halfway, each wall's name fills in as a hint. Most points wins; ties share the podium.

Snake

  • Crossing another player's trail costs a life — you lose your tail and respawn. Your OWN trail is safe.
  • Your tail length = score. Eat glowing slimes for more score and power-ups that spawn around the arena.
  • Make a rival crash and their whole tail bursts into slimes — go gobble them up for points!
  • Power-ups glow around the arena — drive over one to grab it. Use Super Speed, Instant Stop, or Launch to hop over trails.
  • Last snake standing (or top score) wins.

Survival Wave

  • Weave down the lane and stay ahead of the cracking ice!
  • Jump over low walls, or steer through the gaps in tall ones.
  • Fall in the void or drift off the ice and you're out. Last boat alive wins!

Wall Bounce

  • Race down the runway and hit the side ramps to launch at the slime wall!
  • Smack the wall to bounce back — the slime you hit gets knocked out.
  • Bounce hard enough to clear the line in the pool below to score a point. Drop straight in without bouncing and you get nothing.
  • Knock out slime next to an existing hole when you score for bonus points!
  • Most points when the timer runs out wins!
